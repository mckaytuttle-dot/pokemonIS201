Fun Facts

  • The Pokémon franchise was created by Satoshi Tajiri in 1996.
  • There are currently over 900 different Pokémon species.
  • The Pokémon games have sold over 300 million copies worldwide.
  • The Pokémon anime series has been running for over 25 years and has produced more than 1,000 episodes.
  • Pokémon Go, a mobile game released in 2016, became a global phenomenon and has been downloaded over 1 billion times.
